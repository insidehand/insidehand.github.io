MembarCard Privacy Policy

MembarCard

使用條款

MembarCard 提供會員卡片的管理與條碼呈現功能。

輸入的資料會儲存於手機本地之中。

用戶可以透過匯出 JSON 格式的備份檔案來保存資料。

隱私權政策

MembarCard 不會搜集或使用你的任何資訊。

所有輸入的資料都只會儲存在你的手機本地之中，不會上傳至任何伺服器。

權限使用說明

  • 位置 — 用於偵測附近商家，讓相關會員卡自動排到最前面。位置資訊僅在裝置本機處理，不會上傳。
  • 相機 — 用於掃描實體卡片條碼以快速建檔，圖片不會上傳或儲存。
  • 相片 — 用於選取商家照片或會員照，儲存於裝置本機。

應用程式內購買

App 提供選擇性的小費購買功能，付款流程完全由 Apple 處理，MembarCard 不會取得任何付款資訊。

免責聲明

請用戶妥善保存備份檔案，任何資料遺失衍生的問題，MembarCard 概不負責。

聯絡

如有隱私相關問題，請透過 App Store 頁面的開發者聯絡方式與我們聯繫。

Terms of Use

MembarCard provides membership card management and barcode display functionality.

All data entered is stored locally on your device.

You can export your data as a JSON backup file at any time.

Privacy Policy

MembarCard does not collect or use any of your information.

All data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server.

Permissions

  • Location — Used to detect nearby stores and surface relevant cards automatically. Location data is processed on-device only and never uploaded.
  • Camera — Used to scan physical card barcodes for quick entry. Images are not stored or uploaded.
  • Photos — Used to select store or membership photos, stored locally on your device.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional tip purchase. All payments are handled entirely by Apple. MembarCard does not access any payment information.

Disclaimer

Please keep your backup files safe. MembarCard is not responsible for any issues arising from data loss.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us via the developer contact information on the App Store page.
