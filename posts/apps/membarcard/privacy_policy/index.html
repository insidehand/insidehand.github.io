MembarCard Privacy Policy

MembarCard

使用條款

MembarCard 提供會員卡片的管理與條碼呈現功能。

輸入的資料會儲存於手機本地之中。

用戶可以透過匯出 JSON 格式的備份檔案來保存資料。

隱私權政策

MembarCard 不會搜集或使用你的任何資訊。

所有輸入的資料都只會儲存在你的手機本地之中，不會上傳至任何伺服器。

請用戶妥善保存備份檔案，任何資料遺失衍生的問題，MembarCard 概不負責。

Terms of Use

MembarCard provides membership card management and barcode display functionality.

All data entered is stored locally on your device.

You can export your data as a JSON backup file at any time.

Privacy Policy

MembarCard does not collect or use any of your information.

All data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server.

Please keep your backup files safe. MembarCard is not responsible for any issues arising from data loss.
